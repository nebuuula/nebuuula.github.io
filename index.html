Nebula CTF TEAM

About

We are Nebula, a dedicated and vibrant cybersecurity team based in China. Founded in 2018, our team predominantly comprises members from various prestigious universities across the country, all of whom share a passionate commitment to exploring the complex realm of cybersecurity.

With a diverse array of expertise, ranging from software security and binary analysis to web security, cryptography, and AI security, and etc., our members actively strive to unravel the intricate nuances of these fields. We regularly participate in both online and onsite Capture The Flag (CTF) competitions, utilizing these platforms as a means of testing our mettle and honing our skills in a real-world, practical context. Our team’s achievements and rankings in recent events can be found on CTFtime.
